# context
Based on [nyc.gov](https://www.nyc.gov/site/tlc/passengers/your-ride.page) --
- yellow cab are the only allowed vehicles for street-hailing passengers **anywhere in NYC**.
- green cab provides street hailing and pre-arranged services in northern Manhattan and outer boroughs.
